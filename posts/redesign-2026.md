Just completed a redesign of this website for the new year. Hard to believe the last design stuck around for five years.
Really, it's a very moderate reskinning rather than a rearchitecting.

I consider the site's mission in flux. In the past, it has served as a sort of example of my work and I considered a personal website table stakes when applying to be a web developer.

These days, the very idea of web development/software engineering is experiencing extreme upheaval. It was perhaps foolish of me to believe this would be a career path that would last me the rest of my working life; it is clear to me now that it will not be.

Software engineering will condense. There is still a need for engineers and their expertise, with the very important "for now" caveat plumbed in.
